# Kyoto Day Trips

Half-day and full-day escapes when central Kyoto gets too crowded. Pairs with [[Tokyo Itinerary]].

## Arashiyama (Half Day, Go at Dawn)
- The bamboo grove is genuinely better at 7am. By 9 it's a selfie tunnel.
- Walk up to the Iwatayama monkey park afterward. Real climb, real monkeys, almost no one does it.

## Fushimi Inari (Evening)
- Everyone photographs the first hundred gates and turns back. Keep climbing for 40 minutes and you'll have the mountain to yourself.
- Sunset light through the torii is the shot. Bring a headlamp for the way down.

## Nara (Full Day)
- 45 minutes on the Kintetsu line. The deer bow for crackers, then mug you for them. Keep the senbei out of sight until you're ready.
- Todai-ji's Great Buddha is worth the trip on its own. Go before the school groups arrive around 10.

## Uji (Half Day)
- The matcha town. 20 minutes by JR. Byodo-in is the temple on the 10 yen coin.
- Drink the tea at a proper place like Nakamura Tokichi, not a vending machine. The koicha is bitter and intense and the point.

## Getting Around
- Buy an ICOCA card. It works on every train and most buses here.
- Skip Kyoto city buses when you can. They're slow and packed. The trains move.
