# Packing List

The default list I cargo-cult from for any trip under two weeks. Adjust for season and destination.

## Clothing

- 5 t-shirts (merino if I'm rolling carry-on only)
- 2 button-downs
- 2 pants (one nice enough for restaurants)
- 1 light jacket
- Underwear and socks for 7 days
- Swimwear if there's any chance of water
- Sleepwear

## Electronics

- Laptop + charger
- Phone + charger
- Universal travel adapter (the one with USB-C ports)
- Spare USB-C cable
- E-reader

## Toiletries

- Toothbrush, paste
- Deodorant
- Sunscreen
- Small wash bag with a zip
- Earplugs and eye mask — non-negotiable

## Documents

- Passport, ideally with 6 months of validity remaining
- Vaccine card if relevant
- Travel insurance details on phone, screenshotted offline
- Two copies of everything in separate bags

## Things I Always Forget and Then Buy Abroad

- Nail clippers
- A spare phone charger
- Painkillers
- A small lock for hostel lockers

## Things I Used to Pack and Stopped

- Books in physical form. The e-reader replaced them.
- A "just in case" suit. I have never needed one on a trip I planned myself.
- More than one pair of shoes — sneakers cover everything except formal events.
