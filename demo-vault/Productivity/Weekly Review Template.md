# Weekly Review Template

Sunday evening, 30-45 minutes. Cross-references [[Habits I'm Tracking]] and [[Inbox Zero]].

## Looking Back

- What got done this week?
- What didn't, and why? Be specific — "ran out of time" usually means "deprioritized."
- Wins worth remembering. (Real ones, not participation trophies.)
- One thing I'd do differently if I could replay the week.

## Inbox Sweep

- Email — process to zero.
- Slack DMs — respond or archive.
- The physical inbox tray on the desk.
- Browser tabs older than three days. Either capture or close.

## Calendar Audit

- Did I do what was on the calendar?
- What got pushed and is still important?
- Anything next week I should already be preparing for?

## Project Pulse

For each active project: one sentence on the current state, one sentence on the next concrete step. If I can't write the sentence, the project isn't really active.

## Habits Check

Quick pass through the habit tracker. Streaks I want to keep, ones I keep breaking and need to either fix or drop.

## Looking Forward

- Top three priorities for the coming week.
- One thing I'm looking forward to.
- One thing I'm avoiding that I should schedule.
