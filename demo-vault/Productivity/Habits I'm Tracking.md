# Habits I'm Tracking

A short list — long lists collapse under their own weight. Reviewed in [[Weekly Review Template]].

## Daily

- **Morning pages.** Three pages of longhand writing before anything else. Not journaling, just clearing the cache.
- **Movement.** Anything counts: a real workout, a long walk, a bike commute, yoga. The bar is low; the bar is daily.
- **Read 30 minutes.** Phone away. Real book. Not work reading.

## Weekly

- **Weekly review** on Sunday evening.
- **One long walk** of at least 90 minutes, alone, no podcast.
- **Cook two real dinners** — not assembled, actually cooked.

## Monthly

- **Print and review last month's photos.** Ten favorites in a folder.
- **Money review.** What came in, what went out, anything I want to change.
- **One new thing** — a restaurant, a route, a chapter, a person to call.

## What Counts

If I do 70% of the daily ones over a week, that's a good week. The streak isn't the point — the floor is.

## Things I've Stopped Tracking

- **Step count.** Useful as a proxy, useless as a goal.
- **Calorie tracking.** Made me weirder about food than I already was.
- **Screen time.** The number went down, the dependence didn't.
