# Concerts I've Seen

A running log so I stop forgetting. Pairs with [[Albums on Repeat]].

## The nights I'll never forget

- Marah Vell at the Old Chapel - 200 people, no phones up, you could hear her breathe between lines. Best show of my life so far.
- The Glass Atlas at Pier 9 - rained the whole set and nobody left. The lighting through the drizzle was unreal.

## Pleasant surprises

- Cal Renner opening for someone I forgot - I went for the headliner and left a fan of the opener. Bought the record at the merch table.
- A no-name jazz trio at a basement bar - walked in by accident, stayed three hours.

## Good but overhyped

- Senna Voss at the arena - great songs, terrible sound, too far from the stage. Arenas flatten everything.
- Iola at the festival - solid set drowned out by the crowd talking over it. Festivals are not for quiet music.

## Letdowns

- A reunion tour I won't name - they played the hits like a chore. Should've left it a memory.

## What I've learned about going

- Small rooms beat big ones almost every time.
- Get there early for the opener. That's where the surprises live.
- I always think I'll remember the setlist. I never do. Hence this note.
