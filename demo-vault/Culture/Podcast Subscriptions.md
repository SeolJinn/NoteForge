# Podcast Subscriptions

Trimmed several times. The current list is short on purpose.

## Listen Every Episode

- ***99% Invisible***. Roman Mars on design. The episode on the inflatable rats alone justifies the subscription.
- ***This American Life***. Still the best edited audio in the world.
- ***The Rest is History***. Tom Holland and Dominic Sandbrook. Two academics being chatty about everything from Caligula to Cold War spies.

## Listen Often

- ***The Daily*** — NYT. Skip the politics episodes if you can't take it. The non-political ones are very good.
- ***Conversations with Tyler***. Brutal interview style, occasional gold.
- ***Acquired***. Long-form deep dives on companies. Three hours per episode and worth it.

## Listen Sometimes

- ***Reply All*** (defunct, working through the back catalog).
- ***Hardcore History***. Dan Carlin episodes are events, not regular listening.
- ***Sean Carroll's Mindscape***. When the topic interests me I listen; otherwise skip without guilt.

## What I Stopped Listening To

- Anything with a daily news framing. Either the news is a story (in which case dig in) or it isn't (in which case it's anxiety in audio form).
- Most "founder interview" shows. Same questions, same answers, no follow-ups.
- Anything where the host laughs at their own jokes more than the guest does.

## Listening Rules

- 1.0x speed. Faster ruins comedy and softens emotion.
- Skip ads at 30 seconds.
- Skip the host's intro after the third subscribed episode. You know who they are.
