# TV Series Tracker

What I'm watching, abandoning, and lying about catching up on. See [[Movie Recommendations]] for the shorter commitments.

## Currently watching

- *The Quiet Office* (S2) - workplace comedy that's secretly tender. I ration the episodes.
- *Tidewater* - slow-burn mystery, gorgeous coastline, plot occasionally on a smoke break. I'm in anyway.

## Finished and loved

- *Marrow* - three seasons, no filler, stuck the landing. Rare and precious.
- *Northern Lines* - a train-set drama that has no business being as good as it is.

## On pause (will I return?)

- *Crown of Static* - lost me in the back half of S3. The mythology got bigger than the characters.
- *Late Shift* - good show, wrong mood. Shelving it for winter.

## Dropped, no regrets

- *Empire of Glass* - all twists, no people. I stopped caring who lived.
- *The Long Game* - one good idea stretched across ten episodes. Should've been a film.

## Waiting on new seasons

- *Tidewater* S2 - whenever it arrives, I'll drop everything.
- *The Quiet Office* S3 - rumored, unconfirmed, hoped for.

## Rules I track by

- If I'm checking my phone, the show's already lost. I just haven't admitted it.
- Three episodes is the audition. After that it has to earn the rest.
- A perfect ending forgives a saggy middle. A bad ending poisons the whole memory.
