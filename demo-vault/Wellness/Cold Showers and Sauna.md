# Cold Showers and Sauna

My honest take after a winter of trying both, minus the hype.

## Cold Showers
- 30 to 60 seconds at the end of a normal shower. Anything longer and I just dread it and quit.
- The mood lift afterward is real for me. The first ten seconds are awful every single time.
- I breathe slow and steady through it. Gasping makes it worse, the long exhale helps.

## Sauna
- 15 to 20 minutes feels like my sweet spot. Past that I get lightheaded, not relaxed.
- It's the part of my week I look forward to most. More for the calm than any fitness claim.
- I drink water before and after. Skipping that gave me a guaranteed headache.

## Don't Do This After Lifting
- I read that cold right after strength work can blunt the gains, so I save the cold plunge for other days.

## My Verdict
Sauna for relaxation, cold showers for a quick reset. I keep them because I enjoy them, not because of any chart.
