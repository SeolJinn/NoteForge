# Recovery and Rest Days

The part of training I used to skip and the reason I kept getting hurt.

## Why I Take Them
- Muscle gets built on the rest day, not during the session. I had this backwards for years.
- Two hard days back to back and the second one is always worse. The numbers don't lie.

## Active vs Full Rest
- **Active recovery.** Easy walk, light spin, gentle mobility. Keeps me loose without taxing anything.
- **Full rest.** Some weeks I just do nothing, and that's the right call when I'm run down.

## Signs I'm Under-Recovered
- Resting heart rate up 5 to 8 beats in the morning. My clearest early warning.
- Cranky, bad sleep, dreading workouts I usually like. That combo means back off.

## Sleep Is Recovery
- No supplement or stretch beats a solid night. When I sleep badly, I rest harder the next day.

## The Hard Lesson
Pushing through fatigue never made me fitter. It just turned small niggles into weeks off.
