# Mobility and Stretching

The ten minutes I skip when busy and regret within a week.

## Before vs After
- Before training: dynamic stuff. Leg swings, hip circles, arm swings. Never static holds cold.
- After: that's when I do the long holds, 30 to 60 seconds each, muscles already warm.

## My Problem Areas
- **Hips.** Desk job tightens them. Couch stretch, 90 seconds a side, twice a week minimum.
- **Ankles.** Knee-to-wall drills fixed more squat depth than any amount of stretching my legs.
- **Thoracic spine.** Open-book rotations on the floor, ten slow reps each side.

## What I Stopped Doing
- Bouncing into a stretch. Slow and held works, jerking just made things grumpy.
- Forcing range I didn't have. Pain is a stop sign, not a target.

## Honest Note
Mobility work is boring and I never feel like doing it. But the weeks I keep it up, my running and lifting both feel smoother.
