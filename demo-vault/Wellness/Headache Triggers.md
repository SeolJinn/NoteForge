# Headache Triggers

The pattern I pieced together after logging the bad days for a couple of months.

## My Usual Suspects
- **Dehydration.** Far and away my most common one. The afternoon headache is almost always this.
- **Skipped meals.** Going too long without eating reliably brings one on by mid-afternoon.
- **Bad sleep.** Both too little and, oddly, too much on weekends.

## Screen and Light
- Long stretches staring at the screen without a break. The 30-minute look-away helps.
- Harsh overhead light and glare. Dimming things and a warmer screen at night made a difference.

## The Caffeine Trap
- Skipping my usual morning coffee gives me a withdrawal headache by noon.
- But a second afternoon cup wrecks my sleep, which causes one the next day. A narrow window.

## What I Do Now
- Water and a snack first, before reaching for anything stronger. Catches most of them early.

## The Log Helped
Writing down the bad days showed me how often it was just water and food. Boring, but true.
