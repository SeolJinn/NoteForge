# Breathing Exercises

Small tools for when my head is loud. Overlaps with [[Meditation Notes]].

## Box Breathing
- In for 4, hold 4, out 4, hold 4. Four rounds is usually enough to drop my shoulders.
- I use this before anything that makes me nervous. It buys me a calmer first sentence.

## The Long Exhale
- In for 4, out for 6 or 8. The longer exhale is the part that actually settles me.
- Counting matters less than making the out-breath clearly longer than the in.

## Nose Over Mouth
- I try to breathe through my nose during easy runs. Mouth-breathing creeps in when I'm pushing too hard.
- Slower, quieter, deeper. If I can hear myself breathing on an easy day, I'm going too fast.

## When It Doesn't Work
Some days no amount of counting helps and forcing it makes me more wound up. On those days I just walk instead.
