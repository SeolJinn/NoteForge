# Strength Training Basics

The stuff I wish someone had told me before I wasted a year doing random machines.

## The Lifts That Matter
- **Squat, hinge, push, pull.** Almost everything I do is a variation of these four.
- Goblet squats and Romanian deadlifts carried me for months before I touched a barbell.

## Sets and Reps
- 3 sets of 5 to 8 for the big lifts. I leave one or two reps in the tank.
- Going to failure every set just left me sore and unable to train the next day.

## Progression
- Add a little weight when the top of the rep range feels smooth. Patience beats ego.
- I keep a notebook. If I can't remember last week's numbers I'm just guessing.

## Rest
- 2 to 3 minutes between heavy sets. Cutting rest short tanked my numbers.
- Two full sessions a week kept me consistent. Three was better only when sleep was good.

## What Stalled Me
Skipping warm-up sets and chasing a new max every session. Slow, boring, repeatable progress won.
