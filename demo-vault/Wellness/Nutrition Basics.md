# Nutrition Basics

The simple rules I keep coming back to after every complicated diet I tried failed. Fuel for [[Running Form]] lives here too.

## Protein First
- I build each meal around a protein source. Eggs, chicken, beans, yogurt. Everything else fills in around it.
- When I hit enough protein I'm less hungry and snack less. The single most useful habit I have.

## Eat Mostly Real Food
- If it grew or had a face, it's an easy yes. The more a food is processed, the more I treat it as a sometimes thing.
- I don't ban anything. Banning a food just made me want it more.

## Around Training
- A small carb-y snack an hour before a hard run, a real meal after. Running on empty left me flat.
- Long-run days I eat more. Trying to train hard and undereat at once never worked.

## Vegetables
- Easiest win is one vegetable at every meal. I aim for color and variety over any specific rule.

## What I Stopped Doing
Counting every calorie. It made me miserable and I quit within two weeks every single time.
