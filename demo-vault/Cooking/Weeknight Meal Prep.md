# Weeknight Meal Prep

How I eat well on busy nights without cooking from zero every evening. Built around components, not finished meals.

## Cook Components, Not Dinners
Prepping five identical lunches gets boring by Wednesday. Instead I batch a few building blocks and mix them differently each night.

## My Sunday Batch
- A grain: a big pot of rice or farro.
- A protein: a tray of roasted chicken thighs.
- A sauce or two: see [[Tomato Sauce]] and a quick yogurt-herb dressing.
- Washed greens, ready to grab.

## Assembly Nights
Grain plus protein plus sauce plus a fresh element takes ten minutes. Roasted veg from [[Roasting Vegetables]] fills the tray while the grain reheats.

## Lessons Learned
- Store components separately. Dressed salads and saucy grains go soggy by day two.
- Undercook batch grains slightly so they survive reheating.
- Keep three sauces in rotation. Sauce is what stops leftovers feeling like leftovers.
