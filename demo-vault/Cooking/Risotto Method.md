# Risotto Method

The technique matters more than the recipe. Once the method is in your hands you can risotto anything.

## Ratio
- 320g carnaroli or arborio rice
- ~1.2L hot stock, kept simmering on a back burner
- Serves four generously

## Steps
1. Soffritto: sweat finely diced onion in butter, no color.
2. Toast the dry rice 2 minutes until the edges go translucent.
3. Splash of white wine, let it cook off completely.
4. Add hot stock one ladle at a time, stirring, waiting until each absorbs.
5. Around 18 minutes the grain is creamy outside, firm core inside.

## The Mantecatura
Off the heat, beat in cold butter and grated parmesan hard for a minute. This emulsion is what makes it loose and silky, not stodgy.

## Lessons Learned
- Stock must be hot. Cold stock shocks the rice and stalls the cook.
- It should spread like a wave when you shake the pan, never stand in a mound.
- Undercook by a touch and serve immediately. It keeps cooking on the plate.
