# Cleaning Routine

The rhythm that keeps my place from ever getting bad enough to need a whole weekend.

## The daily ten minutes

Small and constant beats big and dreaded, I've proven this to myself.

- Dishes don't sleep in the sink, that's the one rule I never break
- Wipe the kitchen counters before bed
- A quick tidy of whatever drifted onto the floor or table
- Make the bed in the morning, it sets the tone somehow

## Weekly pass

One session, usually a short stretch on the weekend.

- Vacuum the floors and give the bathroom a proper scrub
- Change the sheets and towels
- Take out trash and recycling, wipe the bin if it needs it
- Quick dust of the surfaces that collect it

## The monthly catch-up

These are the things that hide until I go looking.

- Inside the fridge, toss anything questionable
- Behind and under furniture where dust bunnies throw parties
- Wash the things I always forget, like the bin lid and door handles

## What makes it stick

- I keep cleaning supplies where I use them, not in one closet
- Music or a podcast turns the weekly pass into something tolerable
- Doing a little daily means the weekly pass is never overwhelming

The whole trick is never letting it pile up to the scary level.
