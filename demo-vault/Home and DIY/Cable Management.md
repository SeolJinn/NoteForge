# Cable Management

How I tamed the rat's nest behind my desk and under the TV.

## The mess I started with

Behind the desk was a tangle I was genuinely afraid to reach into.

- No idea which cable went to what
- Dust collected in clumps around the knot
- Every time I unplugged one thing I unplugged three by accident

## What actually helped

A few cheap things made the biggest difference.

- Velcro ties instead of zip ties, so I can redo them anytime
- A cable raceway along the desk leg to corral the runs
- A power strip mounted to the underside of the desk, off the floor
- Labels on both ends of every cable, this changed my life

## The labeling habit

Worth its own section because it saved me so much grief.

- A little tag near each plug saying what it powers
- Now I unplug exactly the thing I mean to, every time
- Future me always thanks past me for spending the five minutes

## Keeping it tidy

- Leave a little slack so I can move devices without re-running everything
- Route cables along edges and legs, never straight across open floor
- When I add a new device, I tidy as I go instead of letting it pile up

## The TV setup

- Everything runs into one labeled strip behind the stand
- Excess length coiled and velcroed, not left pooling on the carpet
- A single channel guides the cables down the wall out of sight
